How to Use the Budget App

  1. Set your budget projection by selecting a start date and projection length.
  2. Enter your current account balance.
  3. Add your recurring bills and expenses.
  4. Add your income sources with frequency and start dates.
  5. Review your running budget, expenses breakdown, and lowest projected balances.
  6. Use the edit and delete options to manage your entries.
  7. Export or import your data as needed.
